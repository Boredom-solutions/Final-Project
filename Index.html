Modern-day Problems Need Modern-day Solutions

As we are aware of COVID-19, also known Coronavirus, and the kind of challenges and disasters that it has caused in a single year. World wide, cases are once again rising and forcing countries to tighten restrictions on businesses and edging towards another shut down. In order to prevent the spread, and possible second government-mandated quarantine, every single person need to take percautions and responsibility for limiting the spread of the virus. Accoring to both the World Health Organizaiton and the CDC the best ways to prevent the spread of COVID-19 are as follows:
  • Washing your hands
  • Maintaining a distance of 6ft or more when in public
  • Wearing a mask that covers your mouth and nose when in public
  • Social distancing (staying home and away from people)
All of these are proven to be effective in limiting the spread and preventing people from contracting the virus.
